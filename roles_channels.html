@BrainTrust
The Brain Trust are the stewards of the guild. They ensure that that Captains have everything they need in place to keep the guild moving forward.
Channels:
#brain_trust, #staff_room, #guild_mgmt, #hangout, #strategy, #toolbox
@Captain
This is where the magic happens. Captains are responsible for ensuring that members needs are met. This includes (but is not limited to) recruitment, relaying messages, settling disputes, planning for the future. Each week when the guild assignments are made, Captains are responsible for double checking guild rosters, declaring war, and managing Spin Providers.
Channels:
#staff_room, #guild_mgmt, #hangout, #strategy, #toolbox
@Officer
Officer is an honorary role/title for members that have proved loyalty through time in the guild and consistent/reliable play during wars.
Channels:
none added
@Champion
Champion is an honorary role/title for members that have proved their strength and power by continually scoring high during wars.
Channels:
none added
@Member
Member is the base role for all members.
Channels:
#guild_mgmt, #hangout, #strategy, #toolbox
@Recruit
Recruit is the role for players joining The Brain. Recruits become members after 2-3 weeks/wars.
Channels:
#hangout, #strategy
@Mercenary
Mercenary is the role given to players that are only fighting a single war with The Brain.
Channels:
#hangout, #strategy
@Scout
Scout is a role added to those that provide scouting reports during wars.
Channels:
all war rooms
@Strategist
Strategist is a role assigned to those players that are frequently helping with deck/hero building. It is used mainly to summon them to a channel when members have questions or need help.
Channels:
none added
#brain_trust
This channel is used only to discuss high level management of the guild, such as master logins/passwords and such. Spin Provider login/passwords are also managed here.
Roles:
@BrainTrust
#staff_room
This channel is used to discuss any ideas or pending changes to the guild, along with membership issues that need to be handled "quietly".
Roles:
@BrainTrust, @Captain
#guild_mgmt
This channel is used to discuss the majority of guild management. This is a place where changes are proposed to and by the membership, as well as where any disputes/discussion over management are to be discussed. Any sort of voting / polling on changes should take place here.
Roles:
@BrainTrust, @Captain, @Member
#hangout
This is for general conversation within the guild.
Roles:
@BrainTrust, @Captain, @Member, @Recruit, @Mercenary
#bh_public_chat
This is for general conversation with anybody on the Discord server. THIS INCLUDES NON-BRAIN PLAYERS.
Roles:
@everyone
#strategy
This channel is dedicated to deck and hero building, along with play strategies.
Roles:
@BrainTrust, @Captain, @Member, @Recruit, @Mercenary
#toolbox
This is where we hide our super-secret guild knowledge. :)
Roles:
@BrainTrust, @Captain, @Member
